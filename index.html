Leonard K. Kulisch, M.Sc.

PhD Candidate | Health & Clinical Child Psychology

I am a PhD student and researcher at Ruhr University Bochum and the German Center for Mental Health, interested in Global Family Wellbeing. My research explores how physical health, cognitive factors, and socio-cultural frameworks shape family dynamics. Currently, I study separation anxiety in children across different cultural contexts. In my work, I prioritize innovative research methods and Open Science practices.
